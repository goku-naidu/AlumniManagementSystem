About the University

St Joseph’s University (SJU), a Jesuit institution in Bengaluru, was established in 1882 and became a university on 2nd July 2022. It was inaugurated as India’s first public-private university by President Droupadi Murmu on 27 September 2022.

The IT Department at St. Joseph's University is dedicated to providing cutting-edge technological education and research opportunities.
